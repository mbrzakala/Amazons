# Validator — 1. Idea

Checks: `Hackathon Wiki/stages/1-discovery/steps/1-idea.md`.

## What this step is proving

The team converged on a single, plain-language product direction to point the rest of
discovery at — not a fully-argued pitch, and not yet an AI-necessity justification.

## Evidence to gather

- Wherever the team recorded the sentence (step's `## Result` section, kanban board,
  meeting notes, or Discord/chat log).

## Gate checklist

1. **Idea is written in one plain sentence, not a pitch.**
   Evidence: the recorded sentence. Pass if it is a single sentence describing a
   direction. Fail if it's multiple sentences, or reads like a sales pitch (benefits,
   differentiators, market framing).
2. **No AI-necessity verdict is required here.**
   This item exists to prevent the team from being blocked prematurely — it passes as
   long as nobody demanded an AI-necessity argument before letting this step close.
   Team self-report if unsure.

See `agent_doc/README.md` for the verdict scale and reporting format.
