# 3. Demo Script & Pitch

**What to do:** Prepare the 5-minute pitch: what to show, in what order, and who says what.

**How:**
1. Check the exact pitch rules in the event's Discord doc (time limit, slide policy, who may speak) before preparing anything — don't assume last year's format.
2. Script the demo around the core flow verified in step 2, not every feature built — a hackathon judge remembers the clear story, not the feature count.
3. Assign speaking parts so everyone on the team can speak, per the event rules.
4. Rehearse once against the clock — 5 minutes is short; know exactly where to cut if running long.

**Recommended tool:** Write the script/slides directly; keep the live demo on the deployed URLs (steps 1-2), not localhost, so it matches what judges could check themselves.

## Gate

- [ ] The pitch fits inside the actual time limit, rehearsed at least once.
- [ ] The demo shows the verified core flow, not an aspirational or broken feature.
- [ ] Every team member has an assigned speaking part, per the event's rules.
- [ ] The live demo runs against deployed URLs, not localhost.
