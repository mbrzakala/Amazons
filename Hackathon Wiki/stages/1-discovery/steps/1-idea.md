# 1. Idea

**What to do:** Write the product direction in one sentence. This is a compass, not a
commitment — no AI-necessity test at this task.

**How:** Team states out loud what kind of thing they're pointed at. Write it down.

**Recommended tool:** Say it, write one sentence. Backup: if stuck, ask Claude or
ChatGPT for 2–3 directions inside the hackathon's theme; team picks or adapts one.

## Gate

- [ ] Idea is written in one plain sentence, not a pitch.
- [ ] No AI-necessity verdict is required here — the sentence alone is enough to pass.
