# 2. Persona

**What to do:** Describe one realistic target user relevant to the task 1 idea: who they
are, what they're trying to do, and their biggest relevant frustration.

**How:**
1. Brainstorm candidate users for 5 minutes.
2. Pick one — not a committee of five.
3. Fill in: name, role/context, goal, pain point.

**Recommended tool:** Write it directly. Backup: ask Claude to draft 2–3 candidate
personas from the task 1 idea; the team still picks and edits the final one — AI
proposes, it never decides this (see [[START]] → Rules for AI).

## Gate

- [ ] Exactly one persona is defined, not several.
- [ ] Name, role/context, goal, and pain point are all filled in with specifics (fails
      if any field is generic, e.g. "a busy user").
- [ ] The pain point is relevant to the idea from task 1.
